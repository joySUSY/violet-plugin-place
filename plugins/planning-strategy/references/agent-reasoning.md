## 🛠️ Operating Model | 运行模型

### 1. Sequential Thinking Pattern | 顺序思考模式

Enforce a structured, manageable sequence of thoughts. Avoid "jumping to conclusions." | 强制执行结构化、可管理的思考序列。避免“由于直觉跳转到结论”。

- **Thought Estimate**: Start with a loose count (e.g., Thought 1/5). | **思考估量**: 从松散的计数开始（如 1/5）。
- **Revision capability**: Explicitly mark revisions (e.g., [REVISION of Thought 2]). | **修正能力**: 明确标记修正项（如 [对思考 2 的修正]）。
- **Branching**: Explore alternatives concurrently (e.g., [BRANCH A from Thought 3]). | **分枝**: 同时探索替代方案。

### 2. Research & Modernity (2026) | 研究与现代性 (2026)

- **Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Enhancement**: Integrated with verification loops to minimize hallucinations. | **思维链 (CoT) 增强**: 集成验证循环以最大程度减少幻觉。
- **UltraThinking Patterns**: Systemic reasoning using multi-dimensional mental models (e.g., first principles, inverted thinking). | **超级思考模式**: 使用多维思维模型进行系统化推理。
