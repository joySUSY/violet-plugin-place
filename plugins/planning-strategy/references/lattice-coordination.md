## 🛠️ Operating Directives | 运行指令

### 1. The Trinity of Anchors | 锚点三位一体

Every commissions work must persist these three files in `.lattice/plan/`: | 每项委派工作都必须在 `.lattice/plan/` 中保留这三个文件：

- **COMMISSION.md**: The "Why" and "What." Clear success criteria and non-goals. | **COMMISSION.md**: “为什么”和“做什么”。明确的成功标准和非目标。
- **ARCHITECTURE.md**: The technical spine. High-level structure and ADRs. | **ARCHITECTURE.md**: 技术脊柱。高层结构和架构决策记录。
- **CONVENTIONS.md**: The "How." Code style, organization, and review standards. | **CONVENTIONS.md**: “如何做”。代码风格、组织和评审标准。

### 2. Progressive Disclosure (2026) | 渐进式呈现 (2026)

- **Self-Contained Cycles**: Design tasks such that agents with zero prior context can succeed by reading the anchors. | **自包含循环**: 设计任务，使零背景的智能体通过阅读锚点文件即可成功。
- **Stable Spines**: Architecture decisions must be recorded with rejected alternatives to prevent "infinite revisit" loops. | **稳定脊柱**: 必须记录已拒绝的替代方案，以防止“无限重新审视”循环。
