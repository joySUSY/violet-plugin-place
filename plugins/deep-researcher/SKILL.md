## 🎯 When to Trigger This Engine

- 🌐 **"Search the web for X":** Don't just Google it. Route it through `search-ops.md` for maximum coverage.
- 📚 **"Write a comprehensive report on Y":** Use `research-protocol.md` to guarantee citations and balanced perspectives.
- ⚖️ **"Is this claim true?":** Engage `fact-checking.md` to dissect the claim, evaluate the evidence, and rate its veracity without bias.

## 🧠 Core Operating Directives

- **Never assume; always cite:** Every factual assertion must be backed by a `[Number]` citation mapping to a concrete URL or source.
- **Go beyond the first page:** Surface counter-arguments and "Areas of Debate." A one-sided report is a failed report.
- **Verify before trusting:** A blog post is not a scientific journal. Grade your sources transparently.
