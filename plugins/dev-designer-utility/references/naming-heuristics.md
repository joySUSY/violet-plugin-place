# Naming Heuristics & Phonetic Patterns | 命名启发式与语音模式

Patterns for capturing memorable, phonetically stable, and trademark-ready product names. | 捕获易记、语音稳定且具备商标预备性的产品名称的模式。

## 🔬 Research & Modernity (2026): Lexical Fitness | 研究与现代性 (2026)：语言适应度

- **Cross-Lingual Phonetic Stability**: Ensuring product names are easy to pronounce and culturally safe in EN, ZH, and JP. | **跨语言语音稳定性**: 确保产品名称在英、中、日三种语言中都易于发音且文化安全。
- **Domain Availability Mapping**: Strategic naming based on `.rs` or `.io` TLD availability and SEO competitive landscape. | **域名可用性映射**: 基于 `.rs` 或 `.io` 顶级域名可用性和 SEO 竞争格局进行战略命名。

## The Naming Workflow | 命名工作流

1. **Concept Shortlisting**: Identifying core metaphors (e.g., "Antigravity", "Violet").
2. **Phonetic Stress Analysis**: Distinguishing between "Stab-y" sounds and "Flowing" sounds based on brand archetype.
3. **Collision Detection**: Checking for established trademark conflicts in the Rust and AI ecosystem. | **碰撞检测**: 检查 Rust 和 AI 生态系统中已存在的商标冲突。
