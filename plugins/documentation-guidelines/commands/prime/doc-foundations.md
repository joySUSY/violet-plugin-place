## Documentation Foundations Primer

> Lead with the answer. The reasoning comes after, for those who need it.
> 先给结论。推理过程放后面，给需要的人看。

### BLUF Principle (Bottom Line Up Front)

Every document opens with its most important conclusion. Not background. Not methodology. The answer.
